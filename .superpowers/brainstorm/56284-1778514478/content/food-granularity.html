How detailed should food logging be?

More detail = more accurate BAC, but also more friction to log mid-night.

A

Simple: Meal Toggle

"Did you eat before/during?" — one tap sets a stomach fullness level (empty / light snack / full meal). Applies a single absorption multiplier to BAC. Minimal friction.

B

Medium: Food Type Picker

Log what kind of food — carbs, protein, fatty/greasy, mixed. Each type has a different absorption delay factor. Quick chips-style selector, 5 seconds to log.

C

Detailed: Meal Logging

Log actual meals with estimated calories. Full nutrition-aware BAC model. Most accurate, but similar friction to logging a drink — might feel heavy mid-night.
