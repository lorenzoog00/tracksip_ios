2 — In-Event Food Logging

During the night, log food alongside water with one tap. Each entry resets the decay clock from that moment — eating at midnight counts fully, eating 3 hours ago barely counts.

BAC Model

  • Empty: baseline — current behavior, fastest absorption
  • Snack: −15% peak BAC, 15 min absorption delay
  • Full Meal: −30% peak BAC, 30–45 min absorption delay
  • Decay: effect reduces linearly back to Empty over 2–3 hrs
  • In-event log: resets decay clock from that timestamp forward
