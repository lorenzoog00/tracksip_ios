Instead of: "Stay hydrated and rest."

📅 WEEKLY — Every Monday

One job: Tell the story of the week + one thing to fix

Instead of: "You drank 12 drinks this week, peak BAC was 0.09."

→ "Saturday was the outlier — it drove 60% of your weekly total. Your mid-week nights were actually your best in a month. One fix: the 11pm-to-1am window on weekends is where you accelerate. Try committing to a 1-drink-per-hour rule after midnight."

Trend + anomaly + ONE concrete habit to change.

🗓️ MONTHLY — End of month

One job: Show progress (or regression) + set next month's goal

Instead of: "You had 8 nights out, 45 drinks, 3,200 kcal."

→ "Three weeks improved, one blowout in week 2 skewed everything. Your recovery times are getting faster — that's real progress. Next month: cap yourself at 2 heavy nights maximum and you'll hit your healthiest month in 6 months."

Progress narrative + honest flag + one SMART goal forward.
