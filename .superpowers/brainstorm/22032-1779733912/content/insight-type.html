Pattern detection across time

e.g. "Friday nights are consistently your heaviest — avg 8 drinks vs 4 on other days." Things the user can't see from a single week view.

B

Trend narrative (progress or regression)

e.g. "Your peak BAC has dropped 4 weeks in a row. Here's what changed." Or: "This was your worst week in 2 months — something different happened."

D

Anomaly framing

e.g. "This week was 40% heavier than your 3-month average. One night drove most of it." Puts spikes in context, not just flags them.
