Weekly report — what to add?

On top of: week story + outlier night + one fix. Pick any extras.

W1

Your night of the week

"Saturdays are your night — 80% of your heavy weeks start there." Code detects it, AI says it once and makes it stick.

W2

Hydration grade

Not a number. "Your water game was solid this week — you stayed ahead of it on most nights." Positive when deserved, honest when not.

W3

Recovery speed

"You bounced back faster than last week — something's working." Code compares recovery times, AI narrates the change.

W4

Mixing alert

"You mixed spirits and beer on Saturday — that combo hits harder than sticking to one." Only shows up when mixing actually happened.
