What else could the AI do?

Pick anything that feels worth building. These go beyond the 3 tiers.

A

Pre-night nudge

Before you go out: "Last Friday you had 8 drinks and felt rough Saturday. Tonight, try setting a real stop time — you tend to go hard after midnight." Forward-looking, not backward.

B

"Your signature move"

A pattern the user repeats without knowing it. e.g. "You're a slow starter, fast finisher — your first drink takes forever, then you order three in an hour. That's where your nights get away from you." Named, personal, memorable.

C

Best night breakdown

When someone has a genuinely good night, celebrate it and tell them WHY it worked. "Your Thursday was your best night this month — you ate first, started later, stuck to beer. That's the formula." Positive reinforcement beats lectures.

D

Momentum / streak framing

"Three solid weekends in a row — don't break it this Friday." Short, motivating, shows the AI is watching their arc not just last night. No numbers, just the vibe of progress.

These could live inside the existing tiers or be their own moments. Select what resonates.
